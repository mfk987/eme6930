What Are Educational Games?

Students can have fun and learn at the same time with educational games by the eduGAMER! By learning with our games, students build important social skills while encountering content in fun and engaging ways.

"I learned so much about economics through playing the Auction Game." - Timmy Little, Age 15

Some studie have shown that information retention is greater when students are having fun and interacting with concepts in meaningful ways. Why read a chapter about cells in the human body, when students can play as different parts of the cell in a cooperative game. Which on sounds more fun?

We make life-long learners by making learning fun for all ages! Go to the News section to see our latest games.
